Proposed Scope of Services

Creative & Experience Design

Designing an immersive, high-end environment that elevates the event.

  • Premium Event Conceptualization: Designing an immersive, high-end environment that elevates the flagship event beyond a traditional medical conference.
  • Spatial Design: Strategically planning the layout of the main deck to maximize both the aesthetic appeal and the functionality of the space for networking, dining, and dynamic working sessions.

Vendor & Logistics Management

Sourcing and managing top-tier local providers for furniture, AV, catering and signage.

  • Supplier Sourcing & Supervision: Identifying and managing top-tier local providers for furniture, audiovisual equipment, catering and signage.
  • Custom Solutions (The "Fan" Challenge): Sourcing aesthetic, silent cooling solutions (such as premium tower fans) that blend seamlessly with the upscale decor, avoiding disruptive industrial equipment.

Welcome Kit Sourcing

Curating a high-quality welcome pack that reflects the prestige of the Think-tank.

  • Attendee Gift Upgrade: Curating a selection of high-quality welcome pack, including a sustainable laptop bag (featuring premium eco-friendly brands) that reflect the prestige of the Global Think-tank and offer a sophisticated alternative to standard corporate giveaways.
  • Logistics Management: Full-scale oversight of specialized vendors, streamlining the ordering process, and conducting rigorous quality control through physical sample validation. Our team will personally handle the meticulous assembly and preparation of the 150 premium welcome kits.
  • Badge Logistics & NFC Programming: Oversees the complete logistics for the 150 event badges, ensuring each unit is individually programmed with advanced NFC technology, encoding every badge with the specific data of each attendee.

Entertainment & Ambiance

Elegant background music and live Flamenco performance production.

  • Curated Musical Atmosphere: Selecting and briefing a DJ to provide elegant, non-intrusive background music (e.g., Balearic chill-out/melodic deep house) tailored to the different phases of the event.
  • Flamenco Production: Managing the setup, acoustics, and timing of the live Flamenco performance to ensure an authentic and impactful cultural experience without disrupting the event's flow.

On-Site Coordination & Direction

Dedicated Event Director, logistics management, and precision space transitions.

  • Dedicated Event Director: Providing full in-person supervision throughout the event to act as your primary point of contact and shadow.
  • Logistics & Setup: Managing the early reception, inventory, and strategic placement of signage and accreditation materials.
  • Precision "Room Flips": Executing rapid, seamless transitions of the physical space, specifically the crucial 17:30 dynamic session setup and the 18:30 transformation for the evening reception and Flamenco show.
  • Dynamic Space Management: Our dedicated crew is prepared to instantly deploy a seamless 'Plan B' strategy, ensuring zero disruption to the event's premium experience and schedule in the event of sudden weather changes or last minute requests.

This curated gift set has been thoughtfully selected to offer guests an experience rooted in the pillars of the Mediterranean Diet, globally recognized for its profound benefits on metabolic and liver health. Each element represents the excellence of Spanish artisanal production:
